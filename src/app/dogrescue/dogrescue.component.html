The Project

Setup using Wordpress, this website was built for a local animal rescue that wanted a website to represent their organization. They wanted a basic website to suit the basic needs of the organization, such as displaying their pet listings, a way to display their featured pets, and have general information about the organization. I worked with the organization directly to work towards their website needs and more, including designing and editing all of the images on the website.

The home page is complete with a navigation menu tailored to their needs. There is a dropdown for their pet listings, gallery, contact form, and the adoption application that the organization requires potential adopters to complete.

Additionally, there are links that the organization wanted added that may help guide potential adopters in the adoption process. This includes links to external websites that have information about breeds, what to do when meeting the animal, and what to do when bringing the animal home.

There is also a section for their featured pets. This area is dedicated to two pets that will remain there for a specified amount of time. I edited the images, using Adobe Photoshop, and posted them. I continue to edit and add new images to this section.

Various plugins have been setup and implemented within the website. MetaSlider is used for the main image below the navigation menu, Ninja Forms has been used for the adoption application, and a menu plugin along with a widget for the contact form within the navigation menu.
