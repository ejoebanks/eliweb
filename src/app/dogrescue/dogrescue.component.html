The Project

Setup using Wordpress, this website was built for a local animal rescue that wanted a website to represent their organization. They wanted a basic website to suit the basic needs of the organization, such as displaying their pet listings, a way to display their featured pets, and have general information about the organization. I worked with the organization directly to work towards their needs and more.

There are various plugins that I setup and implemented within the website. One such plugin is the navigation menu

The main page for admins is three columns of appointments, sorted by status. The first column is pending, the second is requested, and the third is for completed appointments. They can choose to accept or deny a requested appointment, which will either just cancel it or move it into the next column. If accepted, it stays marked as "pending" until it has been marked as completed by the admin.

Using Javascript FullCalendar, a calendar is displayed that lists the scheduled appointments. This was done both to give the horse braider(s) a clear view of their appointments, and give the clients a look at any openings. As the majority of work is done overnight, sometimes a certain job may be pushed off until the next morning or one might be done the night before. As such, appointments can be easily dragged and dropped to move them to applicable day. Each cell contains the horse and barn name, and each one is color coordinated to match their status; yellow for requested, blue for accepted, and green for complete.

There are also notifications implemented within the application, with certain triggers. The first one is a simple "new user" notification that is just sent to the admin(s) when a user has made a new account. Another one is an order confirmation of sorts, which is sent to the user who makes an appointment, just notifying them that their request has been submitted. Lastly, there is an appointment reminder that is sent to the client notifying them of their upcoming appointment.
