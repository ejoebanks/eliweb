Project Portfolio

My name is Elijah Banks and I am a recent graduate with a degree in Computer Science. This website, developed in Angular, is meant to display some of my projects, which include those of academic, personal, and professional.

Employee Management

A website custom built with Laravel to handle scheduling within a local branch of Owens Illinois, a glass-container producing company. It allows management to view all of the employees and their shift, job, and other information. Employees are organized by shift where there are options to change their shift, view more information, and more. It also handles notifications & displaying staff events.

GPA Calculator

Commisioned by Aurora University, this GPA calculator was devleoped from scratch. Meetings were held over the course of months to decide features, styling, and use before the final product was hosted on their website. Javascript was used for the calculation functions, hiding and showing elements, and resetting inputs. Styling was done to match the style of the rest of the existing website.

Dog Rescue Organization

Built using Wordpress, this website was designed for a local animal rescue. The organization wanted a simple site that allowed individuals to visit and see basic information about the rescue, links to pet listings on various other websites, a gallery, and ability to send a message. Additionally, there are links to a donation page and a downloadable adoption application that the oranization requires.

Horse Braiding

Developed with Laravel, this website was made for a woman who braided horses for events. She wanted a reliable, effecient way to handle and track her appointments. Clients can visit the website to learn about her services, request appointments, and view their past appointments. The owner can view their requested appointments, accept or deny them, and view all of their accepted and completed appointments.
