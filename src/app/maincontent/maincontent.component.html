More Information

HOPE, a local animal rescue, needed a website to suit their needs. They wanted a simple, straightforward website that had links to what they wanted, images, a contact form, and a way they could post updates about past adoptions.

I set the organization up with web hosting, a domain, and then implemented Wordpress and worked with them on what layout they wanted and how they wanted it to look. I set up the links they provided me, designed certain images, such as the main image and featured pets, and customized the colors to their liking. I installed and set up plugins for certain functions they wanted, such as the contact form that sends the message to the organization leader. I perform regular updates to the design, layout, images, plugins, and more.

Horse Braiding

Developed with Laravel, this website was made for a woman who braided horses for events. She wanted a reliable, effecient way to handle and track her appointments. Clients can visit the website to learn about her services, request appointments, and view their past appointments. The owner can view their requested appointments, accept or deny them, and view all of their accepted and completed appointments.

Employee Management

A website custom built with Laravel to handle scheduling within a local branch of Owens Illinois, a glass-container producing company. It allows management to view all of the employees and their shift, job, and other information. Employees are organized by shift where there are options to change their shift, view more information, and more. It also handles notifications & displaying staff events.
